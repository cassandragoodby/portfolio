#MadebyXD Contest

Dribbble Adobe XD Playoff Contest For this contest I submitted two Adobe XD Projects both inspired by Colorado.

Snapchat Community Geofilter

In October of 2017 I created a Boulder Snapchat Community Filter. As of two years it has been seen on Snapchat 3.8 million times and been used in stories 87.8k. This filter can be used by anyone in the Boulder area through snapchat filter feature.

Snapchat Lens

In June 2019, I created a Augmented reality Snapchat Filter using their software Lens Studio. As of October the lens has just over 1 million views, 705k plays in snapchat and 26k shares.

If you would like to check out this lens, scan the code below using Snapchat by pressing and holding the screen.

Medhacks 2018 Hackathon

Medhacks 2018 was a medical hackathon in September 2018 at John Hopkins University. This project was created in 36 hours and got awarded top ten for the health infrastructure track.
